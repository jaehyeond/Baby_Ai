## THREAT LEVEL REFERENCE

- **5/5**: Directly competes with our core claims. Same mechanism, same domain. Likely rejection if not differentiated.
- **4/5**: High overlap in mechanism or domain. Could threaten novelty if combined with other papers. Must differentiate strongly.
- **3/5**: Significant overlap in one component but overall approach differs. Must cite and acknowledge their approach vs ours.
- **2/5**: Moderate overlap or adjacent topic. Should cite for thoroughness. Our approach is clearly different.
- **1/5**: Minimal overlap or historical reference. Can cite briefly or in future work. Low threat.
